S.S Organic and Herbal Plant is a new generation company serving the industries of organic and agro-food products. We are known as a noted manufacturer and supplier of various types of aloe vera plants. Apart from this, we also manufacture and supply a variety of lemongrass, papaya, safed muesli, shatavari plant, stevia plant and Stevia Leaves. All products we supply are grown in our farms. Only the organic fertilizers are used for keeping the plants safe from weed invasion. Every item manufactured and supplied by us is widely preferred by many people due to pocket-friendly prices and easy availability.
